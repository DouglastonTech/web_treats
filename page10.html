There is the classic chocolate brownie. There are ones with sprinkles, nuts, chocolate chips, and more. We will be making a brownie with chocolate chips.
Ingredients:

2 cups of chocolate chips
1 stick of butter or margarine, cut into pieces
3 eggs
1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
1 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
Add 1/2 cups nuts, if desired
