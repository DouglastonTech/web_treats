There are many different kinds of cakes. There is vanilla, strawberry, red velvet, chocolate, and more. I'm going to tell you how to make a simple chocolate cake.
Ingredients:
2/3 cup of softened butter
1-2/3 cup sugar
3 eggs
2 cups of all purpose flower
2/3 cup baking coco
1-1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1-1/3 cup milk
confectioner's sugar or favorite frosting
Directions:
In a bowl, mix cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
Add eggs one at a time, beating well after every addition.

Combine flour, coco,baking soda, and salt. Add to creamy mixture also with milk, beating after every addition.

Pour batter into a greased and floured 13 in. X 9 in. pan.

Bake at 350° for 35-40 minutes. Let it cool on a wire rack.
After it has cooled, you can add frosting, if you want.
