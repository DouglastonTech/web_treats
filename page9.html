COOKIES
What is your favorite kind of cookie? I love chocolate chip cookies. They are delicious. There are many kinds of cookies like sugar, raisin, nut, and more. I will be teaching you how to make an M&M cookie. Let's get started!
INGREDIENTS:

1 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 heaping cup of brown sugar
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
1/8 teaspoon of salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
2 cups flour
2 cups M&M's
